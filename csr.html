Our CSR Program

Corporate Social Responsibility

KGS Services 's CSR program is devoted to - helping the underprivileged children, supporting old age homes, providing free meals, blood donation and active participation in environmental protection drives. Our CSR initiative has been very thoughtfully named as 'Prayaas' which means "An Effort" of giving back.

We wish to be a company which is respected by the people for its commitment towards social causes. Our employees are motivated to volunteer their time & skills and enjoy the experience of giving back to the communities in which they work. Our endeavor is to see to it that it is a responsibility which must manifest in a thoughtful and deliberate set of initiatives, not just charity.
