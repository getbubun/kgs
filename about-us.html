About Us

Our Story


KGS Services is a global software support provider and, has been providing competent software support service packages to global organizations and independent software vendors. Software product vendors use KGS's offshore software support services for their existing products while using their resources in building newer versions of their products.

We partner with our customer to understand their businesses and provide solutions that deliver the highest levels of quality, service and value as we care about the success of our customers.

We place a high value on establishing long-term relationships with our clients, eventually becoming virtual extensions of their organizations. Our consultants and engineering teams address our clients' specific requirements with best-in-class support solutions across a broad scale of service areas. KGS keeps both strategic and technology partnerships with prominent software providers to supply value-added support solutions ensuing greater return on investments (ROI) for our clients.

KGS Services software support services includes new product installation services, installation of product updates, migrations for major releases of software, other types of proactive services, and support for custom software. Our overall knowledge of the software product is very advance which enables us in resolving the most complex issues in minimal time.

Our highly qualified engineers are available round-the-clock with deep expertise and intense experience in the modern technologies. Customers are our pivot and all our support activities revolve around crafting complete value for their software initiatives.
